Elevation #

To promote certain results over others, you can use Elevation (currently a work in progress).

Why use elevation? #

If you need to put a certain page above another for certain search queries, you should use elevation. It is similar to boosting, but for a result rather than a field.

An example of this is a e-commerce store. As a shop, you want the product pages themselves to appear above the warranty pages, even if they have equivalent relevance scores.

NOTE #

Elevation is currently a work in progress and not yet available for the general public. Have a look at Elevation WIP for more details.
